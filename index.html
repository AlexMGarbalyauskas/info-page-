About the Project

Stay Fit is a final year project designed to revolutionize daily fitness habits. The main goal is to develop a dynamic, user-friendly application that encourages consistent daily activity through an engaging and interactive user experience. By combining fitness challenges, short video submissions, and real-time social interaction, Stay Fit transforms workouts into a shared global experience.

The application creates a fun, social, and motivating environment where users complete quick exercises throughout the day and share their journey with a worldwide community. Whether you're at work, school, or the park, Stay Fit reminds you that fitness is not just about staying healthy - it's about having fun and connecting with others who share your commitment to staying active.

The project aims to promote people to stay fit, active, and healthy while showcasing that you're never alone in your fitness goals. As Charlie Chaplin wisely said, "A day without laughter is a day wasted" - and Stay Fit brings that philosophy to your daily fitness routine!
